Between the events of Path of Exile 1 and 2, the ghostly Einhar Frey and the alchemist Tane Octavius formed an unlikely partnership. Together, they've created an endless cycle of hunting, crafting, and evolution where players hunt corrupted beasts, harvest their organs, and construct personalized boss encounters that unlock powerful crafting options.
Thaumaturgy combines the hunting prowess of Bestiary with the specimen assembly of Metamorph, creating a league mechanic focused on player agency, risk-reward decisions, and deterministic crafting with RNG elements.

TOOLTIP SYSTEM EXAMPLE

Interactive Item References

Using Organ References

Test Subjects can be constructed using various organs. For example, a Purified Crystalline Brain grants powerful spell-casting abilities.

Combining a Purified Hypermetabolic Heart with Purified Unstable Muscles creates an extremely fast and deadly physical attacker.

Unique Endgame Organs

The most powerful organs include Sanctified Tissue , Engorged Amygdala , and Eldritch Mesentery .

Using Currency References

To construct a Test Subject, you'll need Caged Trophies which are dropped by Playmates during the Great Hunt.

Each Caged Trophy increases the Test Subject's power, making the fight more challenging but more rewarding.

Using Atlas Passive References

The Atlas Passive Tree offers powerful customization options. For example, ...Until we slaughter you! adds additional modifiers to Playmate packs.

Risk-takers might enjoy Slow progress is still progress , which introduces crafting failure chance in exchange for organ retention.

For delirium enthusiasts, Capable of experiencing scales delirium intensity with map modifiers.

Organ Tier Comparison

Organs come in three tiers. Compare an Impure Basal Liver with a Refined Basal Liver and finally a Purified Basal Liver to see the progression.
